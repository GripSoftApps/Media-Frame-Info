## Media Frame ## What is this? Media Frame turns a tablet into a clean **Now Playing** screen for your music and video setup. You get: - big cover art and clear text - simple playback controls on tap - a calm idle screen with slideshow and/or clock It is built for landscape Android tablets and works well in a living room, listening room, home cinema room, or store demo setup. --- ## Highlights - **Beautiful now-playing view** Large artwork, readable text, smooth transitions. - **One screen for multiple sources** Works with **Roon**, **Spotify**, **Apple TV**, **Google Cast**, **WiiM** (experimental), **Lyssna själv** (ShazamKit), and **MSI Afterburner** FPS telemetry. You choose source priority in Setup. - **Separate look for music and video** **Utseende → Musikappar** and **Utseende → Videoappar** are independent — music backdrop sliders do not control Apple TV / Plex video backgrounds (and vice versa). - **Optional music video background** For music from Roon/Spotify, you can show a muted YouTube video in the background instead of a static backdrop. - **Simple Apple TV setup flow** Scan, select, pair once with PIN, then use it as a normal source. - **Idle screen that still looks alive** Decorative frame art, slideshow, clock, and a compact connection status area. - **Control from browser / Home Assistant** Built-in LAN web control and REST control are available on Android builds. --- ## Connected apps ### Roon Enable Roon, connect to Core, choose auto/manual zone behavior. ### Spotify - **Last.fm mode** (follow Last.fm now-playing) ### Apple TV Scan devices, pick Apple TV, pair with PIN, then verify connection. ### Google Cast Discover Chromecast / Poll now-playing from connected Cast targets. ### WiiM LAN discovery and now-playing from compatible WiiM streamers (HTTPS to device API). ### Naim LAN discovery and now-playing from compatible Naim streamers (HTTPS to device API). ### Eversolo (EXPERIMENTAL!) LAN discovery and now-playing from compatible Eversolo streamers (HTTPS to device API). ### Shazam Identify what the tablet microphone hears; for example use it as a "source" for vinyl players at home or just when you don't feel like configuriing anything! ### MSI Afterburner FPS and game telemetry from a Windows PC running MSI Afterburner Remote Server ### Home Assistant / Web control Enable local web control and REST control from your network. Useful for automations and external control pages. --- ## Appearance You can style **Music** and **Video** separately. ### Music - choose backdrop source - corner cover position + size - **Album cover style:** **Standard** (flat artwork in the corner) or **Vinyl animation** - title/artist size (**smaller than default** option, default, +10%, +25%), alignment - visualizer options ### Video (Apple TV) - Simple / Advanced layout - Simple: Plex / source poster; Advanced: Fanart - optional app logo in corner - title/episode layout controls - advanced video backgrounds --- ## Screensaver and idle behavior When nothing is playing, you can show: - slideshow - clock - slideshow + clock You can choose image folder, scaling mode, idle delay, and seconds per image.
