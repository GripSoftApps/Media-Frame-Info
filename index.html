What is this?

Media Frame turns a tablet into a clean Now Playing screen for your music and video setup.

You get:

  • big cover art and clear text
  • simple playback controls on tap
  • a calm idle screen with slideshow and/or clock

It is built for landscape Android tablets and works well in a living room, listening room, home cinema room, or store demo setup.

Highlights

  • Beautiful now-playing view Large artwork, readable text, smooth transitions.

  • One screen for multiple sources Works with Roon, Spotify, Apple TV, Google Cast, WiiM (experimental), Lyssna själv (ShazamKit), and MSI Afterburner FPS telemetry. You choose source priority in Setup.

  • Separate look for music and video Utseende → Musikappar and Utseende → Videoappar are independent — music backdrop sliders do not control Apple TV / Plex video backgrounds (and vice versa).

  • Optional music video background For music from Roon/Spotify, you can show a muted YouTube video in the background instead of a static backdrop.

  • Simple Apple TV setup flow Scan, select, pair once with PIN, then use it as a normal source.

  • Idle screen that still looks alive Decorative frame art, slideshow, clock, and a compact connection status area.

  • Control from browser / Home Assistant Built-in LAN web control and REST control are available on Android builds.
